💧 Water pH Awareness & Health Effects
pH Range Health Effects What To Do What Not To Do
< 6.5 Acidic Corrodes pipes, stomach issues, skin irritation, metal leaching Use filtered/bottled water. Test water. Inform authorities Do not drink or cook. Avoid bathing if irritation occurs
6.5 – 8.5 Safe Generally safe for drinking and daily use Use normally. Maintain regular checks Don't ignore sudden taste/color/smell changes
> 8.5 Alkaline Bitter taste, scale buildup, digestive issues, dry skin Use filtered water. Test water. Inform authorities Avoid long-term drinking. Not for infants/elderly

⚠️ Awareness: Report sudden water changes. Contact local water authority if pH is outside 6.5–8.5.
